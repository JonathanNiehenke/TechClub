Yoyos

About

Yo-yos among dolls, balls and kites are the oldest toys. Fundimentally, it uses the rotational motion obtained from going down the string to return back up. Over the years improvements in material and design have greatly improved spin times and stability.

Materials

Yo-yo's began as wood and ceramics. Today, they are from plastics and metals. Since then ball bearings reduce the friction between the string and the axel. The friction/responce system allows for the spinning yo-yo to grip the string and to roll up it. Improvements hide the response system away out of reach of the string until actions force the string to it. Additional, materials like plastics and metals.

Yoyo Styles

Looping

Tricks are centered around flicking the yo-yo back away as it returns so that it never actually enters your grasp. Done repeatedly. Example tricks: Milking the Cow, Punching bag, Shoot the Moon and Three Leaf Clover.

String

Tricks are cenered around landing/restringing the string back into the yo-yo's center. Most common type of trick. Example tricks: Man on flying trapeeze, Double or Nothing, Hudini Mount.

Free-Hand

Preformed with the string attatched to a counter weight that when needed is held. Tricks have the counter weight spinning and moving around just like yo-yo.

Off-String

Preformed with the string not attached to the yo-yo. Tricks have the yo-yo flying beyond the range of the string. A "bind" is preformed for the yo-yo to return. Yo-yo's are usually made of durable plasic lined with rubber.

Yoyo Shapes

Name Image Discription
Imperial The shape focuses the weight nearest the center. Good for looping.
Modified The shape has near elliptical halves, which focues the weight slightly away from the center.
O-Butterfly The shape flares away from the center in a curve. The widened landing gap is easier to thread the string back through. Weight is focused on the rims to increase spin times and improve stability while the yo-yo is threaded and sitting on the string.
V-Butterfly The shape flares away from the center in a straight line. Improves on the O-Butterfly by being more stable when preforming tricks very very quickly. Disadvantages are it does not feel comfortable while being held.
H-Butterfly The shape flares away from the center with a "step". Improves on the other butterfly shapes by having more weight closr to the rims, which impoves spin times. Also it is more stable than the O-Butterfly and more comfortable than the V-Butterfly.
